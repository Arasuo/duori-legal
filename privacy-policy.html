1. Who We Are

Duori ("Duori," "we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered personal budgeting application developed and operated by Nathan Kim. The app is available on iOS and Android and is directed at users in Canada and the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Duori mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Duori, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Your email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Currency preference: Whether you use USD or CAD.
  • Photos and images: If you use the "Can I Afford This?" visual affordability check feature, you may upload or capture a photo of an item. These images are transmitted to our AI processing service and are not permanently stored by Duori after the analysis is complete.
  • Chat messages: Your conversations with Duo, our AI companion, are stored to provide continuity in the chat experience and to allow Duo to reference your financial context over time.

2.2 Financial Information (via Plaid)

When you connect a bank account, Duori uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. ("Plaid") as a third-party intermediary to access your financial data. We do not collect or store your online banking username or password. Through Plaid, we receive and store:

  • Bank account names, types (checking, savings, credit), and masked account numbers (last 4 digits only)
  • Account balances (current and available)
  • Transaction history including merchant names, amounts, dates, and Plaid's categorization
  • Financial institution name and identifier

Plaid access tokens (which allow us to fetch your data from Plaid) are stored in our database in encrypted form using AES-256 encryption. These tokens are only accessible by our backend services and are never exposed to the client application.

2.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version.
  • Push notification tokens: An Expo push token used to deliver spending alerts and Duo messages to your device.
  • Usage analytics: App events such as screens visited, features used, and session duration. We use PostHog for analytics. This data is pseudonymous and used only to improve the product.
  • Crash reports: If the app crashes, diagnostic information may be automatically collected to help us fix bugs.

2.4 Subscription and Payment Information

Duori uses Apple's App Store and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We do not collect or store your credit card number or any payment instrument details. Payments are processed entirely by Apple. We receive only your subscription status (active, expired, trial) and entitlement information.

4. Plaid and Bank Connection

To connect your bank account, Duori integrates with Plaid. When you initiate a bank connection, you will be presented with Plaid's own interface (Plaid Link). Your banking credentials are entered directly into Plaid's secure interface and are never transmitted to or stored by Duori.

Your use of Plaid is governed by Plaid's own Privacy Policy, available at plaid.com/legal/privacy-policy, and Plaid's End User Privacy Policy at plaid.com/legal/end-user-privacy-policy. By connecting your bank account, you agree to Plaid's terms.

Duori's access to your bank data is read-only. We cannot initiate transfers, make payments, or take any action on your accounts. We retrieve transaction and balance data to power the budgeting features of the app.

You may revoke Duori's access to your bank account at any time by disconnecting your bank in the app's Settings screen, or by contacting us at the email below. Revoking access will stop further syncing but will not automatically delete historical transaction data already stored, unless you also request account deletion.

5. AI Features and OpenAI

Duo, our AI companion, is powered by OpenAI's API. When you send a message to Duo or use the visual affordability check, a prompt containing relevant context (such as recent transaction data, account balances, and your message or image) is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing.

This means that a summary of your financial context may be transmitted to OpenAI to generate Duo's response. We send only the minimum data necessary to answer your question and do not include your full transaction history in every request.

OpenAI's data practices are governed by their privacy policy at openai.com/privacy. We use the API under an enterprise agreement that restricts OpenAI from using your data to train their models.

Images submitted for the affordability check are transmitted to OpenAI for vision analysis and are not stored by Duori after the response is delivered.

Important: Duo is an AI assistant, not a licensed financial advisor. Nothing Duo says constitutes professional financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. Always consult a qualified professional for major financial decisions.

6. How We Share Information

6.1 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Duori, our users, or the public.

6.2 Business Transfers

If Duori is acquired, merged, or substantially all of its assets are transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or in-app notice prior to your information becoming subject to a different privacy policy.

6.3 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account and profile data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Transaction data: Retained for up to 24 months from the transaction date, or until account deletion, whichever comes first.
  • Chat messages: Retained until you delete your account or manually clear your chat history in Settings.
  • Spending alerts: Retained for 90 days after delivery.
  • Analytics data: Retained in pseudonymous form for up to 12 months.
  • Images (affordability check): Not stored after the AI response is delivered. Ephemeral.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

8. Security

We take the security of your financial data seriously and implement industry-standard measures including:

  • Encryption of Plaid access tokens at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • All data transmission encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Row-level security on our database so users can only access their own data
  • Authentication tokens stored in iOS Secure Enclave / Android Keystore
  • No storage of banking credentials at any point
  • Backend services accessed only via authenticated API calls with service-role keys never exposed to the client

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.
